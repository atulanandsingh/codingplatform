Technical Events

Inceptum

INCEPTUM is an Inter College Technical Fest. This event is conducted annually. INCEP-TUM 2018 was organized by INCEPTUM Team. Mr.Rohit Anand was the chairperson of the INCEPTUM Committee. Dr.Smriti Agarwal , Dr.Anu Saini and Mr.Jayant Supale were the members of the Organising team. This event was held on 3rd and 5th October 2018. The main motto of this event is to encourage the students of different stream to show their creativity and to perform technical knowledge based task. The students of vari-ous college has shown their interest towards this kind of competition and they have ac-tively participated.

SMART INDIA HACKATHON

Smart India Hackathon, SIH, is an initiative by AICTE, Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India and it involves 1 Lakh+ technical students, 3000+ technical institutions, 200+ organizations from across India. This hackathon consisted of 3 rounds: 1) Technology Students across India submitted their solutions to various problems submitted by ministries and private organizations. 2) Based on the credibility, originality and crea-tivity of their ideas, teams were shortlisted for the next round, where they had to submit a video of the working prototype of their solutions. 3) Based on the videos, competent teams were qualified to the Grand Finale of SIH 2019, to make the prototype amidst industry mentors. Only around 250 teams were selected at national level finale and the details about the institute team which was selected for the grand finale of SIH 2019.

Non-technical Events

Fortius

Fortius is a Latin word which means Faster, Higher and Stronger. This is the Intra College Sports Fest organised every year by the Sports council. The aim of this event is to bring all the students of different stream to come altogether on single platform, perform and show their competence in different sports. The FORTIUS 2019 was conducted from 1st-3rd, February, 2019.

Aarambh

It was felt by the Sports Council in 2018 that an event of state level in Union territory of Delhi must be organised in our campus so that students of GBPEC can interact and compete with other state champions and performers. The event was named as AARAMBH. This was an Inter college competition, which was held from 29-31st, March 2019. Shri Girraj Singh (Chairman of Para Athletics, India) and Ms.Deepika Dhiman(International Karate Champion)graced the occasion and officially started the ‘Aarambh 2019’. Over 50+ institutes from Delhi – NCR participated in this event and cash prizes of ₹ 60,000+ was distributed along with medals and trophies.
